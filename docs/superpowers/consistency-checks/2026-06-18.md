### C1: Feature-doc-gauntlet sign-off self-contradicts on the gating outcome (blocked vs cleared)
**Sources:** Spec sign-off (`...-design.md`) — internal, between its own clauses; cross-checked against Plan Task 5 gating wording.
**A claims (Result / Final disposition, lines 127–128):** "**Result:** FAIL — blocked, paused pending real forecast capture (final human decision, 2026-06-17). NOT cleared for `enate-to-stories`." and "the accepted-limitation override below was offered and initially taken, then **superseded** by the Feature owner's decision (2026-06-17) to **pause** ... The Feature stays blocked until Seam 2's forecast success path is grounded by a real capture and the gauntlet returns a clean automated pass."
**B claims (Accepted limitation, line 136; run-2 line, line 139):** "On that decision the Feature **is cleared for `enate-to-stories`**." and "Clearance for `enate-to-stories` **stands** on the human acceptance above."
**Likely authority:** A. The Result line and Final disposition are explicitly the *final* human decision (2026-06-17) and explicitly *supersede* the earlier accepted-limitation override; the surviving B clauses are residue of the superseded override and now read as a live clearance they no longer grant.
**Suggested resolution:** Reword the "Accepted limitation" paragraph and the run-2 "Clearance ... stands" line so they record the override as **superseded/withdrawn** and state the Feature is **NOT cleared** — bringing them into line with the Result/Final disposition (FAIL, blocked) and with Plan Task 5's hard-stop gate. Leave a single, unambiguous gating outcome.

*Note on the Seam 2 ↔ sign-off ↔ Plan Task 5 axis specifically called out for this review:* Seam 2 (c)/(d)/(e) ("success shape NOT YET GROUNDED", proof gated to Plan Task 5) is mutually consistent with Plan Task 5's 🚧 GATING / BLOCKING Step 1 hard-stop, and both are consistent with the sign-off's **Result: FAIL — blocked**. The only break on this axis is internal to the sign-off (C1): its "cleared for enate-to-stories" clauses contradict its own "NOT cleared / blocked" final disposition. Seam 2 and Plan Task 5 do not contradict each other.
